HOW TO PLAY

Survive the void. Outgun the swarm.

🕹
Move — Arrow keys / WASD on desktop, drag the left stick on mobile.
🔫
Fire — Spacebar or hold the FIRE button. Your ship auto-aims forward.
💠
Power-ups — Collect glowing orbs: shields, rapid-fire, spread-shot, and hull repair.
🪙
Coins — Destroy enemies to drop coins. Chain kills quickly to build a combo multiplier.
👹
Bosses — Every few sectors, a Void Sentinel blocks your path. Learn its patterns and strike weak points.
⏸
Pause — Tap the pause icon or press ESC anytime.
